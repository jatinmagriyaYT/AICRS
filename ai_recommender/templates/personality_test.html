About the MBTI Assessment

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) helps identify your natural preferences in four key dimensions. Your results will help us match you with careers where you're likely to thrive and find fulfillment.

Extraversion (E) / Introversion (I)
Where you focus your attention and get energy
Sensing (S) / Intuition (N)
How you take in information
Thinking (T) / Feeling (F)
How you make decisions
Judging (J) / Perceiving (P)
How you deal with the outside world

How to Complete This Assessment

  • Answer each question based on your natural preferences, not what you think you "should" choose
  • There are no right or wrong answers - be honest about your tendencies
  • Choose the option that feels most true to you, even if it's not always true
  • Complete all questions to get accurate results
